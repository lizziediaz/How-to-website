Directions

To make the actual waffles, follow these steps:

1. Preheat your waffle iron

2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl until fluffy

3. Beat in the flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, until smooth.

4. Spray the preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.

5. Pour the mix onto the hot waffle iron.

6. Cook the waffle until golden brown.
